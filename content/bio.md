I am a PhD student in the School of Computer Science and Engineering at the University of Electronic Science and Technology of China. My research focuses on multimodal large models, fine-grained cross-modal alignment, image-text retrieval, and knowledge graph link prediction.

My recent work studies how to bridge the semantic gap between dense visual signals and sparse text supervision. I am also interested in practical AI system deployment, especially adapting vision and semantic models to domestic accelerator platforms such as Huawei Ascend.

I have experience in research projects on AI computing chips, business-service large language models, knowledge graph reasoning, and open-source database compatibility.
